COMMUNITY IS IMPORTANT TO US

A successful Hungry Jack’s is a hub of the community it serves. The Hungry Jack’s team is united in our passion to give something back to those communities.

HELPING COMMUNITIES

MAKING THE WORLD BETTER

At Hungry Jack’s better service is more than a great in-store experience, we’d also like to make the world outside our restaurants a better place. And with over 400 stores and 17,000 employees across every state, we’ve got the power to do a lot of good.

MAKE-A-WISH

Hungry Jack's Crew, Managers, Franchisees and Head Office staff are united in our commitment to raising funds to help bring hope, strength, and joy to critically ill children and their families. Our partnership with Make-A-Wish® allows us to help make vital wishes come true for really sick kids around Australia.

TARONGA ZOO

Hungry Jack’s supports Taronga Zoo’s dedicated team to present the very best of the natural world to Sydney-siders and guests from around the world.

AUSSIE AUSSIE AUSSIE!

GETTING IN THE GAME

We don’t just sponsor sport, we participate in growing it, from grass roots to elite levels.

THE AUSTRALIAN NBL

Basketball is one of the fastest growing sports in Australia, and Hungry Jack's has played a big part in making that happen. In 2017 we renewed a relationship which began thirty years ago, when we were one of the NBL’s first naming rights partners. Basketball is all about the fans, so we do whatever it takes to deliver a seriously exciting spectator experience.

WEST COAST EAGLES

The first Hungry Jack's store was in the WA suburb of Innaloo, so it’s hardly surprising that we’re West Coast Eagles fans from way back. The Hungry Jack’s logo’s been on the Eagle’s Guernsey for all their premierships, and in 2017 we renewed our commitment to support them – making the bond between these two great WA brands stronger than ever.
