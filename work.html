JOIN AS A

CREW MEMBER

First jobs don’t come much better than this. Learning communication & teamwork skills in a buzzing, friendly environment. And the hours have flexibility to fit in with school, family or sports commitments. You’ll work hard, but you’ll laugh a lot, and you’ll never be short of chances to show your potential.

Crew Member Roles:

  • Front Counter Serving our Guests with passion and precision
  • Kitchen area Mastering the multi-tasking of food preparation
  • Drive Thru Learning the value of fast, efficient customer service

What we look for in a Crew Member:

Putting customers first and being a natural and self-motivated team player. Having a willingness to learn and follow instruction. Being friendly and smiling comes easily!

JOIN AS A

RESTAURANT MANAGER

This is the role which empowers you to make decisions and motivate your team to deliver the best service to our customer. Our work environment is fast paced so we will need people that enjoy being busy and challenged at work.

Restaurant Manager Responsibilities:

  • Restaurant performance
  • Maintaining the highest standards of food service & delivery
  • Recruitment, training and development of crew

What we look for in a Restaurant Manager:

Energy, self-motivation, leadership and good communication skills. Taking pride in achieving goals without cutting corners or compromising standards.

JOIN AS A

FIELD SUPPORT

State Field support teams spend much of their time on the road, providing the leadership, coaching and mentoring needed to ensure that every Hungry Jack’s store and crew is always at their best.

Field Support Roles:

  • Multi Restaurant Managers – supervisory role for more geographically remote store locations
  • District Managers – leads and coaches 8+ stores including the Multi Restaurant Managers to operationally achieve key objectives.
  • State Human Resources – working alongside our field support team, providing both best in class training and safety, equipment processes and employee relations.
  • State Maintenance – servicing repairs all equipment to ensure the very best delivery of our menu to customers.

What we look for in Field Support:

A customer-first commitment. Self-confidence and leadership in driving business results. A talent for teaching, coaching and inspiring.

WHY SHOULD YOU WORK WITH US

GIVE BACK

At Hungry Jack’s we don’t just want to make our customers happy. We also want to give something back to the community that supports us. So be a part of something bigger, like make-a-wish, sports and community event sponsorships and volunteering.

FLEXIBILITY

With over 420+ restaurants in Australia, chances are you’re not far from a Hungry Jack’s in Australia. And the hours we offer range from casual, part-time or full-time, varying from early morning to late at night. So you can balance it with sports, and school commitments – and a hectic social life, of course!

BE REWARDED

Hungry Jack’s staff perks include staff food discounts, service recognition, access to employee assistance programs and heaps of workplace flexibility.
